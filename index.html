World Class MS Profile Gas Cutting Solution

Batul Engineering started profile cutting in 1980 , Batul Engineering have been servicing all over India market since more than 40 years Successfully and have plenty of experience in the domain of Mild Steel Profile services.
Profile cutting is an in-depth process that takes a piece of steel and transforms it into a particular shape. Cutting is typically carried out on a table using a machine that is suitable for the product. Machine types vary as there are a number of different kinds of metals available in various thickness and grades, so not all can be cut the same way.

We would like to register with you as a Vendor for supplying the all kind of Mild Steel products like Mild Steel HR Plates, CR Sheet Checkered plates, Mild Steel Circle, Mild Steel Rings, Mild Steel Flange Ms Pipes ,MS Square tube, and Profiles template based cutting services including Grade E 250 E 350 IS 2062 make Tata , JSW, Essar, Namco and SAIL from Mumbai & Pune.
